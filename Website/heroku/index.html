    Sentiment Anaylisis Tones Team

Twitter data has proven to be invaluable resource in the extraction of situational awareness information relating to any crisis. This has been demonstrated even further with the current world wide pandemic. Our team's goal was to design a sentiment analysis model to evaluate the Twitter data in order to determine the overall feeling of a subject, in this case, Coronavirus - COVID-19. As of July of 2020, more than thirteen million people have been diagnosed with the Coronavirus known as COVID-19, and half a million people have already lost their lives due to this infectious disease. The World Health Organization (WHO) declared the COVID-19 outbreak as a pandemic on March 11, 2020. Since that declaration, social media platforms have experienced an rise in the content related to the pandemic. This is where our investigation began.
